What is MTrack?

MTrack is a personal finance application I originally built for my own use. Over time, it became stable and useful enough to share publicly.

Unlike most finance software, MTrack does not use the cloud. All your data is stored locally on your computer in simple CSV and JSON files. There is no account, no tracking.

Key Features

  • Consolidated dashboard for income, expenses, and investments
  • Multi-currency support with correct locale formatting
  • Automated recurring entries (salary, SIPs, EMIs)
  • Goal tracking with fund allocation
  • Historical analysis with visual breakdowns
  • All data stored locally (no internet required)
